The ocean breeze puts my mind at ease.

Colibra island is one of the best kept secret of dasol pangasinan, Best place to visit is early morning when waters are calm. The front part of the beach is the best place to snorkel with clear, warm water, live and beautiful corals, plenty colorful school of fishes . The sand is very fine, soft, creamy looking, beach is clean. Please pick up your own trash and don't leave any trace behind to preserve this beautiful place. Need to hire a private boat from one of the resorts in Dasol to go to Colibra Island
